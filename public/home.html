Features
EVERYTHING YOUR GYM NEEDS
Comprehensive tools covering every aspect of gym operations — from member onboarding to equipment maintenance.
👥
Member Management
Full CRUD for members — assign plans, trainers, track status, emergency contacts, and plan expiry dates.
💰
Payment Tracking
Record payments with auto-generated receipt numbers. Track Cash, UPI, Card transactions and manage pending dues.
💪
Workout Plans
Trainers assign day-wise exercises with sets, reps, weight, and instructions. Members view their full programme.
📅
Class Scheduling
Schedule group classes with trainer assignment, capacity limits, and member self-enroll/unenroll.
📏
Body Metrics
Track weight, BMI, body fat %, muscle mass, and measurements. Visual progress charts for every member.
🏋️
Equipment Inventory
Manage gym equipment with condition tracking, purchase dates, and maintenance schedule alerts.
📊
Analytics Dashboard
Revenue charts, attendance trends, plan distribution, and KPI cards — all updated in real time from the database.
🎯
Goal Tracking
Members set personal fitness goals with target dates. Track Active, Completed, and Cancelled goals.
📢
Announcements
Post gym-wide or targeted announcements for Members or Trainers with expiry date support.
